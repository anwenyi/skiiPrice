Suggested Usage:
-Use after you cleanse and tone, but before you target and moisturize.
-Use daily, morning and night, to maximize results.
-Facial Treatment Essence is safe for use around the eyes, including on the eyelid itself.
-Pour the Facial Treatment Essence onto a cotton pad or into the palm of the hand. Use about five to 10 splashes, enough to soak the cotton pad or palm completely.
-Apply to both face and neck, pressing gently and evenly into skin until the essence has soaked in.
-Tap the skin lightly to enhance absorption.
